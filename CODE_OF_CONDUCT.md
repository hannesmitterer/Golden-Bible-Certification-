# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct (Summary)

Our community is dedicated to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone.

Reporting
If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainers in a direct message on GitHub (user: hannesmitterer) or open an issue labeled "conduct" so the maintainers can respond.

Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported and will be reviewed by the maintainers, who will determine appropriate actions.

For the full text, include an extended Code of Conduct or a link to the Contributor Covenant as desired.
